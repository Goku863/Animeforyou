Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

AnimeVault is designed to be privacy-friendly. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to save your preferences and watchlist. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.
  • Analytics: We may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes pages visited, time spent, and general location (country level).
  • Cookies: We use only essential cookies required for the site to function. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

2. How We Use Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to optimize content
  • Ensure the site functions properly
  • Prevent abuse and maintain security

3. Data Storage

All your data (watchlist, preferences, bookmarks) is stored locally in your browser. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on our servers. If you clear your browser data, your preferences will be reset.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site may use third-party services for:

  • Font Delivery: Google Fonts for typography
  • CDN: Cloudflare for content delivery and security
  • Hosting: Cloudflare Pages for site hosting

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

5. Children's Privacy

AnimeVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository or by opening an issue.
