How we handle your data

Home/Privacy

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve your experience. This includes anonymous usage analytics, search queries, and bookmarked content stored locally in your browser. We do not require account registration and do not collect personal identifiable information unless voluntarily provided through contact forms.

How We Use Your Data

Usage data helps us understand which features are most popular and how we can improve the platform. Search queries help refine our recommendation algorithms. Bookmark data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers unless you choose to sync across devices.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for video embedding and analytics. These services may collect anonymized data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete any data associated with your use of this platform. Since we don't require accounts, most data is under your direct control through your browser settings. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us via our contact page.
